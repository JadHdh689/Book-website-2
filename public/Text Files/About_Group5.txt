🌿 Positive CBT Collection

Ages 18+

“Think Well, Live Well”

This adult-focused collection offers structured support through Positive Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). It is designed to help individuals:
	•	Recognize and reframe negative thought patterns
	•	Improve emotional regulation and coping skills
	•	Build a more empowered, resilient, and hopeful mindset
	•	Develop practical strategies for everyday challenges

Each exercise in this group is grounded in scientific research and designed to be actionable, reflective, and personally meaningful.

Recommended Duration: Self-paced, based on personal goals or therapeutic guidance.


📽 Each book includes a short video accessible via QR code, offering visual support and guidance for key concepts.
