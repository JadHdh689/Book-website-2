🔵 Tiny Tools for Big Feelings

Ages 13–17

“Becoming My Best Self”

This stage focuses on identity-building, self-confidence, and preparing for future goals.
Teens are guided to explore who they are, understand their emotions, and grow into their best selves with purpose and clarity.

This group helps teens:
	•	Reflect on their strengths, values, and aspirations
	•	Build confidence through journaling and guided prompts
	•	Learn how to regulate emotions in complex situations
	•	Strengthen resilience and self-leadership
	•	Develop a growth mindset for adulthood

Recommended Duration: 1 year of structured self-reflection and emotional development.
