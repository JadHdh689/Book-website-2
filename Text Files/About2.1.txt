To Stay Focused and Calm is the first title in the Tiny Tools for Big Feelings series, thoughtfully designed for children ages 6 to 8.

This book helps children:
	•	Develop focus and attention
	•	Calm themselves in everyday situations
	•	Understand and manage their emotions

It is ideal for:
	•	Parents and caregivers
	•	Educators and specialists
	•	Schools and therapeutic centers

The book includes:
	•	Engaging stories
	•	Guided activities using drawing, movement, and reflection
	•	Practical strategies to manage distractions and build emotional awareness

All content is based on Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) and Positive Psychology, creating a joyful and structured path to emotional growth.
